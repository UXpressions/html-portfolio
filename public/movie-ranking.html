The Best Movies According to Christian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Forrest Gump

It's an emotional rollercoaster and beautifully illustrates the law of attraction. It shows the power of being a genuinely good person.

The Matrix Series

Really cool sci-fi movie that plays on the psychological idealogy of being indoctrinated to a system while encouraging viewers to break free from it.

Scareface

I love a good gangster flick and this is the best! Not to mention it includes a story of starting from the bottom and working your way to the top.
